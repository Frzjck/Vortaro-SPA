Welcome to Vortaro

This page has been designed for autodidactic purposes and as a proof of concept. It is dedicated to people who want to expand their knowledge of foreign language vocabulary.

It allows to store words you wish to learn, organize them by groups, practice via different exercises and keep track of your learning progress.

My Vocabulary

This is your glossary, here you can find all your stored words. Here is where you can manage your words and groups by adding, editing and deleting.

Even thou group sizes are limitless, we recommend you not to exceed twenty words per group to keep your exercises manageable.

To create or edit a word you are given four inputs:

• Word - for foreign word.

• Translation - here you input a particular translation you wish to learn.

• Tips - optional field, here you can write clues about word meaning, so in case you completely forget meaning of the word during exercise you can have a refresher.

• Additional translations - another optional field, here you can add supplementary translations that will be counted as correct answers during exercises.

• To see additional translations of the word, press on the eye icon.

• To see tips, hover over question icon.

On "My Vocabulary" page you also can track your progress.

• Next to each group you can find rating bars that represent average grade of words in that group.

• Next to each word you can find a star that represents your knowledge of that word.

Rating bars and starts are color-coded, red signalling need for attention, orange - average and green means you have a solid grasp.

Settings

Settings give you access to multiple theming options under palette icon and learning modes under lightbulb icon.

Also, here you can change translation direction. Depending on which direction you have selected you can be practicing meaning of the foreign word or its spelling.

There are also two different modes you can choose to do exercises.

Choose mode you like before selecting an exercise.

Quiz is recommended for getting familiarized with new words.

Spelling is targeted to improve Orthography.

Keep in mind spelling mode is the only mode that will allow you to earn scores higher than average.

Exercises

Currently there are three types of exercises available.

view_list "By group" it allows you to exercises all words located in a group.

"Random" throws at you 15 random words designed as refresher.

sync_problem "Error correction" allows you to redeem words with worse scores by giving you an exercise of 15 words with the worse score.
